title: Expand

Expand shortcode can help to decrease clutter on screen by hiding part of text. Expand content by clicking on it.

## Example

### Default

<!-- prettier-ignore-start -->
```tpl
{{</* expand */>}}
## Markdown content
Dolor sit, sumo unique ...
{{</* /expand */>}}
```
<!-- prettier-ignore-end -->

{{< expand >}}

### With Custom Label

<!-- prettier-ignore-start -->
```tpl
{{</* expand "Custom Label" "..." */>}}
## Markdown content
Dolor sit, sumo unique ...
{{</* /expand */>}}
```
